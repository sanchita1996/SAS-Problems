Problem Statement:
You are given two datasets: ORDERS and CUSTOMERS.

ORDERS contains order details, and CUSTOMERS contains customer information.
Some customers may not have placed any orders.

Task:
Create a dataset that shows all customers along with their order details.
If order details are missing, display ORDER_ID as . and AMOUNT as 0.

---

Create Dataset:

data orders;
input ORDER_ID CUSTOMER_ID AMOUNT;
datalines;
101 1 500
102 2 700
103 3 400
;
run;

data customers;
input CUSTOMER_ID NAME $;
datalines;
1 Amit
2 Neha
3 Raj
4 John
;
run;

---

Input Table: ORDERS

## ORDER_ID | CUSTOMER_ID | AMOUNT

101      | 1           | 500
102      | 2           | 700
103      | 3           | 400

---

Input Table: CUSTOMERS

## CUSTOMER_ID | NAME

1           | Amit
2           | Neha
3           | Raj
4           | John

---

Expected Output:

## CUSTOMER_ID | NAME  | ORDER_ID | AMOUNT

1           | Amit  | 101      | 500
2           | Neha  | 102      | 700
3           | Raj   | 103      | 400
4           | John  | .        | 0
