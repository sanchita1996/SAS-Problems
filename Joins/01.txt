Problem Statement:
You are given two datasets: EMP and DEPT.

EMP contains employee details, and DEPT contains department names.

Task:
Create a dataset that shows each employee along with their department name.

---

Create Dataset:

data emp;
input EMP_ID NAME $ DEPT_ID;
datalines;
1 Amit 10
2 Neha 20
3 Raj 30
4 John 40
;
run;

data dept;
input DEPT_ID DEPT_NAME $;
datalines;
10 HR
20 IT
30 FIN
;
run;

---

Input Table: EMP

## EMP_ID | NAME  | DEPT_ID

1      | Amit  | 10
2      | Neha  | 20
3      | Raj   | 30
4      | John  | 40

---

Input Table: DEPT

## DEPT_ID | DEPT_NAME

10      | HR
20      | IT
30      | FIN

---

Expected Output:

## EMP_ID | NAME  | DEPT_ID | DEPT_NAME

1      | Amit  | 10      | HR
2      | Neha  | 20      | IT
3      | Raj   | 30      | FIN
4      | John  | 40      | .
