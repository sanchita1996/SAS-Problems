Problem Statement:
You are given two datasets: SALES and PRODUCT.

SALES contains transaction details, and PRODUCT contains product information.
Some sales records may not have matching product details.

Task:
Create a dataset that shows all sales along with product name.
If product name is missing, display it as "Not Available".

---

Create Dataset:

data sales;
input SALE_ID PRODUCT_ID QUANTITY;
datalines;
101 1 2
102 2 1
103 3 5
104 5 3
;
run;

data product;
input PRODUCT_ID PRODUCT_NAME $;
datalines;
1 Laptop
2 Mobile
3 Tablet
;
run;

---

Input Table: SALES

## SALE_ID | PRODUCT_ID | QUANTITY

101     | 1          | 2
102     | 2          | 1
103     | 3          | 5
104     | 5          | 3

---

Input Table: PRODUCT

## PRODUCT_ID | PRODUCT_NAME

1          | Laptop
2          | Mobile
3          | Tablet

---

Expected Output:

## SALE_ID | PRODUCT_ID | QUANTITY | PRODUCT_NAME

101     | 1          | 2        | Laptop
102     | 2          | 1        | Mobile
103     | 3          | 5        | Tablet
104     | 5          | 3        | Not Available
