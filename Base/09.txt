Problem Statement:
You are given a dataset containing FULL_NAME values with:

* Multiple extra spaces between words
* Leading and trailing spaces

Task:
Create two new variables:

1. CLEAN_NAME → keep only a single space between words (remove extra spaces)
2. NOSPACE_NAME → remove all spaces completely

---

Create Dataset:

data names;
input FULL_NAME $50.;
datalines;
Amit     Sharma
Neha      Verma
Raj   Singh
John        Doe
Ankit  Kumar   Singh
;
run;

---

Input Table: NAMES

## FULL_NAME

Amit     Sharma
Neha      Verma
Raj   Singh
John        Doe
Ankit  Kumar   Singh

---

Expected Output:

## FULL_NAME               | CLEAN_NAME           | NOSPACE_NAME

Amit     Sharma       | Amit Sharma          | AmitSharma
Neha      Verma         | Neha Verma           | NehaVerma
Raj   Singh           | Raj Singh            | RajSingh
John        Doe         | John Doe             | JohnDoe
Ankit  Kumar   Singh    | Ankit Kumar Singh    | AnkitKumarSingh
