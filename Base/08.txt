Problem Statement:
You are given a dataset containing employee transactions across different departments.
Each employee may have multiple transactions within the same department.

Task:
For each DEPT, keep only the **first occurring employee record**.

---

Create Dataset:

data emp_dept;
input DEPT $ EMP_ID NAME $;
datalines;
HR 1 Amit
HR 2 Neha
IT 3 Raj
IT 4 John
FIN 5 Ravi
FIN 6 Kiran
;
run;

---

Input Table: EMP_DEPT

## DEPT | EMP_ID | NAME

HR   | 1      | Amit
HR   | 2      | Neha
IT   | 3      | Raj
IT   | 4      | John
FIN  | 5      | Ravi
FIN  | 6      | Kiran

---

Expected Output:

## DEPT | EMP_ID | NAME

HR   | 1      | Amit
IT   | 3      | Raj
FIN  | 5      | Ravi
