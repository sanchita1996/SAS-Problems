Problem Statement:
You are given two datasets: EMP and SALARY.

EMP contains employee details, and SALARY contains salary information.
Some employees may not have salary records.

Task:
Create a dataset that shows all employees along with their salary.
If salary is missing, show it as 0.

---

Create Dataset:

data emp;
input EMP_ID NAME $ DEPT $;
datalines;
1 Amit HR
2 Neha IT
3 Raj FIN
4 John MKT
;
run;

data salary;
input EMP_ID SALARY;
datalines;
1 50000
2 60000
3 55000
;
run;

---

Input Table: EMP

## EMP_ID | NAME  | DEPT

1      | Amit  | HR
2      | Neha  | IT
3      | Raj   | FIN
4      | John  | MKT

---

Input Table: SALARY

## EMP_ID | SALARY

1      | 50000
2      | 60000
3      | 55000

---

Expected Output:

## EMP_ID | NAME  | DEPT | SALARY

1      | Amit  | HR   | 50000
2      | Neha  | IT   | 60000
3      | Raj   | FIN  | 55000
4      | John  | MKT  | 0
