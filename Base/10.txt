Problem Statement:
You are given a dataset containing EMAIL values.

Each email follows the pattern:
username@domain.com

Task:
Create three new variables:

USERNAME → text before "@"
DOMAIN → text between "@" and "."
EXTENSION → text after "."

Create Dataset:

data emails;
input EMAIL $50.;
datalines;
"amit.sharma@gmail.com"
"neha_verma@yahoo.in"
"raj.singh@outlook.com"
"john.doe@company.org"
;
run;

Input Table: EMAILS

EMAIL

"amit.sharma@gmail.com"
"neha_verma@yahoo.in"
"raj.singh@outlook.com"
"john.doe@company.org"

Expected Output:

EMAIL | USERNAME | DOMAIN | EXTENSION

"amit.sharma@gmail.com" | amit.sharma | gmail | com
"neha_verma@yahoo.in" | neha_verma | yahoo | in
"raj.singh@outlook.com" | raj.singh | outlook | com
"john.doe@company.org" | john.doe | company | org
