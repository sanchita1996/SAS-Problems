Problem Statement:
You are given two datasets: STUDENTS and MARKS.

STUDENTS contains student details, and MARKS contains subject scores.
Some students may not have marks records.

Task:
Create a dataset that shows all students along with their marks.
If marks are missing, display them as 0.

---

Create Dataset:

data students;
input STUDENT_ID NAME $ CLASS $;
datalines;
1 Amit 10A
2 Neha 10B
3 Raj 10A
4 John 10C
;
run;

data marks;
input STUDENT_ID MATHS SCIENCE;
datalines;
1 80 75
2 90 85
3 70 65
;
run;

---

Input Table: STUDENTS

## STUDENT_ID | NAME  | CLASS

1          | Amit  | 10A
2          | Neha  | 10B
3          | Raj   | 10A
4          | John  | 10C

---

Input Table: MARKS

## STUDENT_ID | MATHS | SCIENCE

1          | 80    | 75
2          | 90    | 85
3          | 70    | 65

---

Expected Output:

## STUDENT_ID | NAME  | CLASS | MATHS | SCIENCE

1          | Amit  | 10A   | 80    | 75
2          | Neha  | 10B   | 90    | 85
3          | Raj   | 10A   | 70    | 65
4          | John  | 10C   | 0     | 0
