Problem Statement:
You are given a dataset containing FIRST_NAME, LAST_NAME, and CITY.

Task:
Create a new variable FULL_INFO by concatenating:
FIRST_NAME, LAST_NAME, and CITY with a space separator using CAT functions.

---

Create Dataset:

data emp;
input FIRST_NAME $ LAST_NAME $ CITY $;
datalines;
Amit Sharma Delhi
Neha Verma Mumbai
Raj Singh Pune
John Doe Chennai
;
run;

---

Input Table: EMP

## FIRST_NAME | LAST_NAME | CITY

Amit       | Sharma    | Delhi
Neha       | Verma     | Mumbai
Raj        | Singh     | Pune
John       | Doe       | Chennai

---

Expected Output:

## FIRST_NAME | LAST_NAME | CITY    | FULL_INFO

Amit       | Sharma    | Delhi   | Amit Sharma Delhi
Neha       | Verma     | Mumbai  | Neha Verma Mumbai
Raj        | Singh     | Pune    | Raj Singh Pune
John       | Doe       | Chennai | John Doe Chennai
